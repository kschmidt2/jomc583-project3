Strikeouts and Fastball Velocity in MLB, 1998-2015

Strikeouts have been on the rise in Major League Baseball over the past few seasons as more power arms throwing harder than ever enter the league. Led by pitchers such as Aroldis Chapman in the bullpen (98.8 average fastball velocity) and Stephen Strasburg in the rotation (95.5), pitch velocity has been on the rise since Pitch f/x began tracking the data in 2002. View the chart below, which shows strikeout data since the last MLB expansion in 1998, to see how the rise in strikeouts correlates with the rise in fastball velocity.
